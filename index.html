Athinkra's Bamum Unicode font and input development.

Fonts

Athinkra fonts

  1. JG Bamum Akauku Sans Unicode
  2. JG Bamum Akauku Calligraphic Unicode
  3. JG Bamum Akauku Mono Unicode

The Athinkra Bamum fonts were designed by Jay Glavy for Athinkra and are released under the OFL 1.1 license. These fonts only support the Bamum block. They contain Graphite, OpenType and AAT support.

Other fonts

Noto Sans Bamum supports the Bamum and Bamum Supplement blocks
